# Aphorism for Design

## Pattern: aphorism-for-design

Extends: aphorism
Apply aphorism form to design docs

## Mechanism: Modular stanzas

One stanza, one idea
New stanza when subject shifts
Stanzas stand alone and compose

## Mechanism: Labeled factors

Labels mark stanza role
Problem, mechanism, constraint, unknown, tradeoff, rationale, etc
Labels are suggestions
New labels emerge from the work
Unlabeled stanzas carry narrative

## Mechanism: Identifiers

Patterns, modules, design elements have slugs
Stanzas have titles
Reserve ids for definable elements

## Mechanism: Story progression

Stanzas are narrative beats
Each beat earns the next
No recap, no restatement

## Mechanism: Agent collaboration

Write to extent of confidence
Volume signals convergence
Restraint signals divergence
Ask or wait when shape is unclear

## Constraint: Form demonstrates form

This pattern is written in its own form
Design docs should be also
