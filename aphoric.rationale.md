# Aphoric Form — Rationale

## Problem: prose hides reasoning

Prose flows, smooths, connects  
Weak claims survive inside filler  
Disagreements hide in hedges  
Readers drown before they decide

## Problem: agents default to prose

Agents generate prose by construction  
Coherence is hoped for, not enforced  
Implicit assumptions accumulate  
The reasoning is never quite visible

## Problem: no shared form for human and agent

Humans think in fragments, then pad  
Agents pad by default  
No shared discipline, no shared checkpoint  
Misunderstanding compounds silently

## Rationale: why short lines

Bandwidth reduction forces clarity  
Filler dies first under the constraint  
Then weak claims  
What remains is load-bearing

Prose hides disagreement in hedges  
Aphoric form forces the claim flat  
Mismatches become visible

## Rationale: why stanzas

Parallel ideas in prose blur together  
Stanzas force order  
Order forces priority  
Modularity enables composition

## Rationale: why titles

Titles surface the structure  
Named sections enable navigation and meta-discussion  
Untitled stanzas preserve narrative flow  
Two levels — chapters and sections — scale without nesting

## Rationale: why epistemic state is explicit

Uncertainty hidden in caveats is invisible  
Hedges and questions given their own lines become checkpoints  
Unresolved tension stays unresolved  
False conclusions are worse than open questions

## Rationale: why story, not schema

Schema lists features, flat  
Story has tension, movement, stakes  
The reader feels the problem before seeing the mechanism  
Design emerges through progression, not enumeration

## Rationale: agent output as signal

Volume reveals convergence  
Restraint reveals divergence  
Silence is information  
Inverts the default failure mode of generation

## Rationale: form as discovery

Form does not merely display thought  
Form generates factoring  
Stanzas reveal structure by the act of writing them  
The discipline is the method, not the skin
