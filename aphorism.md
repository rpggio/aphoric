## Aphorism Form

Each line one utterance
Claim, question, command, or hedge

Use hedges to express uncertainty
Use open questions to express tension
Do not resolve what should stay open

No line longer than twelve words, roughly
New stanza when the subject shifts
One stanza, one idea

No connective filler
Juxtaposition does the work

The line break is the pause
Punctuation only when meaning requires it

Every word must earn its place
Cut adjectives first
Then cut verbs that only link
Use abbrev often

State each step
Omit the transitions
Let order imply relation
